The calculaor is inspired by the calculator app design on LG G3. It's a rather minimalistic design.

The buttons of the calculator are registered with clicking event and respective keypress events.

The calculation is done with the `eval` function.

  • Use Node.innerHTML to modify the content of an existing DOM element.
  • Add title bar for the calculator app, the title bar will be consisted of icons that add functionalities including close, minimize and restore.
  • Add a gradient-based design of the calculator.
